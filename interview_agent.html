🎤 AI Interview System

Customer-Facing Professional Position Screening

📋 Before You Begin

  • Find a quiet space with minimal background noise
  • Test your microphone and ensure it's working properly
  • Have your resume handy for reference during the interview
  • Prepare specific examples of your work experience and achievements
  • Research our company - be ready to explain why you're interested
  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace - the AI needs to understand you

🤖 What to Expect

  • Duration: 10-15 minutes
  • Format: The AI will ask 4-5 core questions with possible follow-ups
  • Questions will cover: Work experience, customer service scenarios, company knowledge, problem-solving approach
  • Be specific: Give concrete examples rather than general statements
  • Ask questions: You'll have a chance to ask about the role
  • Recording: This interview will be recorded and reviewed by a hiring manager
Status: Ready to Start

This interview will be analyzed for communication skills, relevant experience, and cultural fit.
A hiring manager will review your responses and contact you with next steps.
